Timeline

  1. 1978: Kobe born in August 23, 1978 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania
  2. 1996: Bryant was drafted by the Charlotte Hornets. He was traded to the Los Angeles Lakers
  3. 1997: He is champion of the Slam Dunk Contest.
  4. 2000-2002: Won three Finals in a row of the championship series(Three-peat).
  5. 2003: Bryant was charged with one couont of sexual assault on a 19-year-old female working at an hotal in Colorado. He admit that he was guilty of an adultery but innocent for the rape charge. The case against him was dismissed in 2004.
  6. 2007: Bryant won the gold medal at the FIBA Americas Chanpionship in Las Vegas.
  7. 2008: Won gold medal at Olympics Games in Beijing. He also won an NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP) title the same year.
  8. 2012: Bryant won the gold medal at Olympics Games in London
  9. 2016: He retire from NBA. He announced it via The Player's Tribune the November 29, 2015.
  10. 2020: Kobe died due to an helicopter crash the January 26, 2020. He was with is daugther Gianna and seven other people who passed away too. He died at the age of 41.
